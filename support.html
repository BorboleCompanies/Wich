Frequently Asked Questions

How does the randomization work?
Wich uses your device's built-in random number generator to produce a fair, unbiased result every time.

Is my data being collected?
No. Wich makes no network requests, uses no analytics, and collects zero data. Everything stays on your device.

Where is my history stored?
Decision history is held in temporary memory only. It clears when you close the app.

Can I suggest a new decision mode?
Absolutely. Send us an email and we'll consider adding it in a future update.
