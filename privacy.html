Wich - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Overview

Wich ("the App") is a random decision-making utility developed by Borbole Companies. Your privacy matters to us, so we built the App to work entirely on your device with no data collection of any kind.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or location. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. We do not make any network requests. The App functions entirely offline. Decision history is stored only in temporary memory and is cleared when the App is closed.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect data from any user, including children. It is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@borbole.com.
